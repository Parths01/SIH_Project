Frequently Asked Questions

How quickly can we implement College ERP?
Implementation typically takes 2-4 weeks depending on your institution size and customization requirements. We provide complete support throughout the process.
Is training provided for staff members?
Yes, we provide comprehensive training for all user roles including administrators, faculty, staff, and students. Training includes both online sessions and documentation.
Can we migrate data from our existing system?
Absolutely! We support data migration from most existing systems including Excel files, other ERP systems, and databases. Our team handles the entire migration process.
What kind of support do you provide?
We provide 24/7 technical support via phone, email, and live chat. This includes system maintenance, bug fixes, regular updates, and user assistance.
Is the system mobile-friendly?
Yes, College ERP is fully responsive and works seamlessly on all devices including smartphones, tablets, and desktops. Mobile apps are also available.
How secure is our data?
We use enterprise-grade security including data encryption, secure servers, regular backups, and compliance with educational data protection standards.
